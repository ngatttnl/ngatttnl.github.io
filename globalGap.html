GlobalG.A.P

Introduction

Export markets for agricultural commodities requires certification to demonstrate compliance to importing country and customers criteria. International Standards that are GFSI approved (www.mygfsi.com) are most widely accepted which includes GLOBALG.A.P (www.globalgap.org). GLOBALG.A.P includes; food safety, hygiene, environmental concerns, animal welfare and worker welfare. GLOBALG.A.P is well recognised both by domestic and export markets and is suitable for fruit and vegetables, livestock, aquaculture and tea and coffee.

Ayebee Quality (established 2005) provides training, auditing and consulting to achieve GLOBALG.A.P certification. We can assist from application for certification, through each step to achieve GLOBALG.A.P certification. We can then provide ongoing support as needed to maintain GLOBALG.A.P certification.

Producer group certification

Co-operative or similar producer groups may consider option 2 producer group certification.

This involves a central Quality Management System (QMS) and a robust internal audit process. The QMS and a sample of the suppliers are externally audited. This can save on certification costs.

Expertise is shared, and training and internal audits can be managed centrally.
